## 1. About

ECR Inn Beach House (E.C.R. INN @ Belle Rive) is a private beach-house (villa) retreat in Paramankeni, near Chennai, on the East Coast Road (ECR), Tamil Nadu — just meters from the sea. It hosts families, groups of friends, and business delegations all year round.

The property has three fully furnished, two-storeyed beach houses (named villas include Jasmine Villa and Lotus Villa). Each villa is rented as a whole house, giving guests complete privacy. Brand promise: "More than a Beach House… an experience." Core values: exclusive, peaceful, safe, and hygienic.

## 3. Rooms & Accommodation

Each of the three beach houses is a fully furnished two-storeyed building with private balconies and open terrace access. **Each villa is rented as a whole house — not as individual rooms.** Each villa has three bedrooms; every bedroom has a King-size bed, an LED TV with dish channels, and an attached western bathroom with geyser.

**Ground floor:** furnished reception hall (LED TV, dish channels); spacious dining hall and living area; 1 bedroom (King bed, LED TV, attached western bathroom with geyser); a separate service bathroom for drivers; optional modular kitchen (utensils, cutlery, gas connection, RO water).

**First floor:** 2 bedrooms connected by interior stairs (each with King bed, LED TV with dish channels, attached western bathroom with geyser, and a private balcony); spacious hallway; access to the open terrace.

## 4. Tariff & Pricing

Pricing is **per house, per day**. Transparent pricing — no hidden costs or brokerage commission.

- **Tariff:** Rs. 16,000 per day, per house.
- **Regular accommodation:** 10 persons per house.
- **Additional person (with bed):** Rs. 500 per person.
- **Maximum occupancy:** up to 5 extra persons beyond the regular 10 — i.e. maximum 15 persons per house.
- **Cooking facility (optional):** fully furnished kitchen (gas, stove, utensils, cutlery, refrigerator, RO water). Guests may cook themselves or hire a South Indian style cook. Groceries and the cook's charges are borne by the guest, plus Rs. 1,500 per day for kitchen maintenance and gas.
- **Seafood:** freshly caught fish, lobster and shrimp available at extra charge.
- **Boating to the island:** available at extra charge, paid directly to the respective agency.

**Payment:** 50% advance confirms the booking. The remaining balance is paid at check-out during final settlement. Cooking charges are settled at the end; boating is paid to the agency.
